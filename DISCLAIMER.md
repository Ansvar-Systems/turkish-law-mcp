## Data Authority and Limitations

### Source Authority

This Tool aggregates data from official Turkish legal databases. However:

| Source Type | Authority Level | Use Case |
|------------|----------------|----------|
| **Official government databases (Resmî Gazete, Mevzuat Bilgi Sistemi)** | Authoritative | Statute text (primary reference) |
| **Community/open-data sources** | Supplementary | Legal summaries, cross-references (verify independently) |
| **International treaty databases** | Authoritative for international law | International obligations metadata |

**CRITICAL**: Community-maintained resources are **NOT** official government publications. While generally reliable, they may contain transcription errors, outdated information, or incomplete coverage.

### Commercial Legal Databases

For professional legal work, **always cross-check with commercial legal databases** such as:

- Kazancı İçtihat Bilgi Bankası, Lexpera, Sinerji Mevzuat

These services provide editorial oversight, comprehensive annotations, and professional-grade currency guarantees that this Tool **cannot** provide.

## Currency and Staleness

### No Real-Time Updates

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by **weeks or months**
- Turkish law changes continuously through new legislation (kanunlar, kanun hükmünde kararnameler, cumhurbaşkanlığı kararnameleri), amendments, and court decisions
- **Last-updated timestamps** in tool responses indicate data age, but should be treated as unreliable

### Staleness Warnings

The Tool includes staleness warnings when data is >30 days old. **These warnings are not exhaustive**:

- Even "fresh" data may be outdated if recent amendments were published in the Resmî Gazete
- Staleness warnings do not guarantee completeness or accuracy
- Always check official sources for the current legal position

### Amendments and Repeals

- A provision marked "in force" (yürürlükte) may have been:
  - Amended (değiştirilmiş) after the last database update
  - Repealed (yürürlükten kaldırılmış) but not yet removed from the database
  - Subject to transitional provisions (geçici maddeler) affecting its applicability
- The Tool does **NOT** automatically track amendments in real-time

## Coverage Gaps

### What's Missing

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Complete case law (içtihat)** -- Coverage is limited; do not rely solely on this for case law research from the Yargıtay, Danıştay, or Anayasa Mahkemesi (Constitutional Court)
2. **Historical statute versions** -- Limited availability of historical provision wording (önceki metinler)
3. **Legal commentary (doktrin)** -- No editorial annotations, academic commentary, or practice guides
4. **Unpublished decisions** -- Lower court decisions and administrative rulings largely absent
5. **Full legislative history** -- Limited coverage of parliamentary debates (TBMM tutanakları) and legislative rationale (gerekçeler)
6. **Secondary legislation** -- Coverage of regulations (yönetmelikler), communiqués (tebliğler), and circulars (genelgeler) may be incomplete

### Impact on Professional Use

These gaps mean:

- **Incomplete Legal Research**: Tool results are inherently incomplete and may miss critical authorities
- **Context Missing**: Without commentary and legislative history, interpretation may be incorrect
- **Secondary Legislation Blind Spots**: Turkey's extensive secondary legislation may not be fully covered
- **International Law Blind Spots**: Cannot fully assess international treaty obligations or compliance
